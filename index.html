Features

Everything You Need to Run Your Hostel

Powerful tools and features designed specifically for modern hostel management, from room allocation to financial reporting.

Room Management

Smart room allocation with real-time availability, automatic assignments, and detailed room analytics. Support for multiple room types and configurations.

Student Directory

Comprehensive student profiles with contact info, room assignments, payment history, and document storage. Quick search and filtering.

Billing & Payments

Automated invoicing, online payment processing, expense tracking, and financial reports. Multiple payment gateway integrations.

Maintenance Tracking

Streamlined complaint management with priority levels, status tracking, and automated notifications to maintenance staff.

Announcements

Broadcast important notices, events, and updates to all residents. Pin critical announcements and track read receipts.

Analytics & Reports

Detailed insights into occupancy rates, revenue trends, student demographics, and operational efficiency with exportable reports.
